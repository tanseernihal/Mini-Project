How to Sign In

Follow these steps to sign in to your College Timetable account:

  1. Go to the Sign In page.
  2. Enter your **Email ID** and **Password**.
  3. Click on the **Sign In** button.
  4. If you forgot your password, click on **Forgot Password?** and follow the instructions.
  5. Once logged in, you will be redirected to your **Timetable Dashboard**.

If you are facing issues, contact support@college.edu.
